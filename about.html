Want to know who am i?

My name is João Vitor Marra, a software engineer. I'm 18 years old, and i am based at Belo Horizonte, MG, Brazil, my hometown!

I started into software development in september of 2021, when i still was in the high school. The reason to this early interest was cause i'm the most curious people ever, and i like to search and study things, and since there, my focus was at programming. Since there, i reached so many goals that i had. I learned all the basics and foundations at pogramming and web development, and then i was capable to won a financial aid from Coursera and Edx, and that made possible to have access to high level contents at computer science and technologies, including a Professional Certificate from Harvard and another one from Stanford, at edx platform.

In january of 2022, i already had some projects using vanilla JavaScript, React and bootstrap, and with that, i landed my first job at a Aeronautic engineering company, where i started to use Wordpress to maintain and change two websites. Besides the job, i also made a lot of projects in 2022, not just to the Front-end but also back-end and full-stack applications, using Python, Flask, Django, ReactJS, Angular, MeteorJS, REST APIs, and some other features.
